Final Year Project - DRIV - Driver Drowsiness Detection

The Driver Drowsiness Detection App (DRIV) is an Android application I developed as part of my final year project at Dublin City University. Leveraging facial recognition technology, the app monitors drivers for signs of fatigue, such as prolonged eyelid closure or head movements indicative of drowsiness. When drowsiness is detected, the app provides real-time alerts to ensure driver safety and prevent accidents. This project showcased my ability to combine technical knowledge, problem-solving skills, and user-focused design to create a practical solution to a critical road safety issue.
